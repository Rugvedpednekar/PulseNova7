Privacy & Data Use

Clear rules for web uploads, Alexa, and consent.

Web uploads

X-rays and lab reports are uploaded via the web app only. Alexa does not support uploads.

Alexa limitations

  • Alexa can do symptom conversations and basic “find care” prompts.
  • Alexa can’t upload images or PDFs.
  • If you want to reference labs/scans, PulseNova will ask you to open the web app.

Medical disclaimer

PulseNova provides guidance, not diagnosis. If you have severe symptoms (chest pain, trouble breathing, confusion, fainting), seek emergency care immediately.
